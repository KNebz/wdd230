Our History

Bountiful Foods was founded in 1999 by Meredith Thomas right here in Southern California. Meredith began to be very health conscious once she started college and noticed the options for healthy but delicious food was limited. She knew there were others like her that wanted a refreshing drink, more flavorful than water but didn't have the excessive sugars and unhealthy ingredients of most available drinks. She spent months testing out different healty mixtures but nothing met her high standard of quality. That was until one day in 1999 she returned home from shopping at the local farmers market with a bag of various fruits. She slipped on her wet kitched floor, spilling and damaging her fruit. The fruit made a mess broken and splattered on the ground. As she bent down to pick up the fruit she slipped again, face first into the fruit concoction. The taste was amazing and she knew from that moment on that fruit juice was her future. She promptly sold off her fledgling stocks in Amazon and bought her first food truck and the rest is history.
